Community-built tool for regenerative filmmaking

It is designed for filmmakers, makers, repair caf√©s, fab labs, and anyone who believes tools should be shared, adaptable, and owned by the community.

**Version:** 0.1.0

---

## Why this exists

This project was created out of a simple conviction:

> Technical tools should serve people, not extract value from them.

Professional filmmaking accessories are often overpriced, locked behind proprietary systems, or designed to be replaced rather than repaired. This tool offers an alternative: generate your own follow focus rings, adapt them to your lenses, and fabricate them locally.

No accounts.  
No subscriptions.  
No telemetry.  
No platform lock-in.
